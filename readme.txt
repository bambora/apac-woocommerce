Welcome to the Bambora APAC Online Plug-in for WooCommerce

== Description ==

Welcome to the Bambora APAC Online Plug-in for WooCommerce. 
Bambora Online is a Payment Service Provider now available in Australia and New Zealand, providing payment solutions for all types of businesses. We offer functional, flexible e-commerce solutions to meet your every need. Whether you want a secure solution that is quick and easy to implement in your online store, or a fully integrated, customised solution that meets your specific needs, Bambora has the answer.

https://www.bambora.com/en/nz/online-payments/products/bambora-ready/
https://www.bambora.com/en/au/online/overview/

= Features =
* Receive payments securely through the Bambora Online Checkout payment window
* Receive payments securely through the Woocommerce payment page via our API integration
* Store Credit Cards securely via our Customer registration/ tokenisation method
* Get an overview over the status for your payments directly from your WooCommerce order page
